What is AAC?

Try Out AAC For Yourself!

Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) is, simply, anything people use to help them communicate. For many people it can be a device or flipbook that they use to geneate speech, or to share thoughts or ideas.

If you've never seen someone use AAC before then it might be surprising at first, but everyone deserves ways to express themselves, and to be listened to and respected by those around them -- and AAC can be fun!

We are committed to helping Utahns -- and everyone -- understand just how much of a difference AAC can make. We want to support everyone, regardless of where they are on their AAC journey.

AAC in the Cloud - The Power of One

AAC in the Cloud is an annual online conference that is available - for free - and is focused on everything related to AAC! There are a variety of topics and levels of expertise covered by presenters, and previous years' sessions are kept online for review as well.

This year's theme is The Power of One. We invite you to take part in this AAC focused, online conference with sessions addressing a large variety of topics. Whether you're new to AAC or a seasoned veteran, there are great resources available to help improve your ability to support AAC users in growing their own indepence and autonomy.
